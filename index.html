What I Do

After I inherited the analogue Nikkormat camera from my Granddad, I discovered how much fun it is to shoot on film. I just love to stroll through the city and try to capture any precious moment I encounter. A couple of years ago I also fell in love with the Minox 35GT , the amazing "pocket camera".

Who I Am

I am Roos, a 28 year old photography lover. A few of my favourite photographers are Vivian Maier, Berenice Abbott, Sebastião Salgado, Stephan Vanfleteren, Brassaï, Robert Doisneau, Bruce Davidson, and Ed van der Elsken. Whenever I'm not out with my camera, I aim to accelerate the energy transition.
